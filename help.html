About RegisCore

RegisCore is an intelligent university registration and scheduling platform designed to simplify the academic planning process for students, faculty, and administrators.

The platform helps students manage course registration, monitor degree progress, generate schedules automatically, track deadlines, communicate with advisors, and stay updated through notifications and calendars.

Website Functionalities

Dashboard

Displays student progress, GPA, upcoming sessions, oral examinations, and quick academic statistics.

My Progress

Tracks completed credit hours, remaining requirements, completed courses, and required future courses.

Registration Advisor

AI-powered schedule generator that recommends the best semester plan based on completed courses and preferences.

Course Catalogue

Displays all required courses for the student's major including details, prerequisites, instructors, and materials.

Shared Calendar

Shows lectures, deadlines, holidays, office hours, oral sessions, and registration events.

Notifications Center

Provides important updates regarding registration, deadlines, GPA changes, and advisor recommendations.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI advisor work?

The advisor analyzes your completed courses, prerequisites, preferences, and credit limits to generate an optimized schedule.

Can I edit the generated schedule manually?

Yes. After generating a schedule, students can modify, add, or remove courses manually.

How can I check missing prerequisites?

The system automatically checks prerequisites and highlights missing requirements before registration.

Can I export my progress report?

Yes. Progress reports and completed course records can be exported as PDF files.

How do notifications work?

Notifications are automatically sent for deadlines, oral sessions, advisor updates, and registration alerts.

Can I access the calendar on mobile devices?

Yes. RegisCore is designed to work across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.
